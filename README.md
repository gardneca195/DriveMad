# Drive Mad — Claude Games

A physics-based driving/balancing game in the style of *Drive Mad*. Balance your
car across tricky obstacle courses and reach the checkered flag without flipping
over or smashing your driver's head.

**100% offline. No ads. No tracking. No internet required.**

This is an original clone built from scratch (custom 2D physics engine, no
external libraries) — it does not use any of Poki's copyrighted game files.

## How to play

- Double-click **`Play Drive Mad.bat`** (Windows), or just open **`index.html`**
  in any web browser.

### Controls
| Action | Touch | Keyboard |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Drive forward | tap **►** | → or **D** |
| Reverse / brake | tap **◄** | ← or **A** |
| Restart level | ↻ button | **R** |
| Level select | ≡ button | — |

## Goal
Reach the flag. Don't flip over, don't let the driver's head hit the ground,
and don't fall into a gap. 10 levels of increasing madness, with seesaws,
ramps, gaps, and stacks of boxes. Your best times are saved locally.

## Files
- `index.html` — the whole game (open this to play)
- `Play Drive Mad.bat` — Windows one-click launcher
